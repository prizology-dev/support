---
title: "How to Submit a Prize"
description: "Learn how to submit a Prize to the Prizology database"
---

If you represent a medical society, foundation, or organisation and are interested in partnering or collaborating with us, please send a partnership enquiry to **[support@prizology.net](mailto:support@prizology.net)**. Our team will be happy to assist in featuring your prizes on Prizology.

<Note>The ability for users to directly add prizes is a feature currently in development and will be available in the future.</Note>
