---
title: "How to Report an Error"
description: "Help us keep Prizology accurate and reliable"
---

If you notice incorrect information, broken links, missing images, or any other issue on the site, please send an error report to support@prizology.net. Our team will review your message and work to resolve the issue promptly to help keep Prizology accurate and reliable.

<Note>The ability for users to directly report errors is a feature currently in development and will be available in the future.</Note>
