## How to contact us

For any questions or support, please email us at **[support@prizology.net](mailto:support@prizology.net)**

Before contacting us, please search through our support articles to see if they can solve your query. 

When emailing, please clearly state the subject of your query in the email subject line. Please allow us up to 5 working days to respond.
