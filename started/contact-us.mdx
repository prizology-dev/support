---
title: "How to Contact Us"
description: "Get in touch with the Prizology team"
---

Before reaching out, please review our support articles to see if your question has already been answered.  

If you still need assistance, contact us at **[support@prizology.net](mailto:support@prizology.net)**. We aim to respond to all queries within five working days, and we appreciate your patience while we review your message carefully.

When emailing, clearly state the subject of your query and include as many relevant details as possible to help us resolve your issue efficiently.

<Note>Please send the email from the email address associated with your account. Otherwise, we may not be able to provide support.</Note>
