## Google Sign-Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Head to our sign-up page">
    Go to https://prizology.net/login to start the sign-up process.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Press "Google"'>
    Click the "Google" login button to continue with your Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Google account">
    If prompted, sign in to Google and select the account you'd like to use with Prizology.

    <Warning>
      You may see the address `ocogkamlpprubpxhwmql.supabase.co `during this process—don’t worry, this is normal and we’re working with Google to update it to display our organisation’s name.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Prizology" icon="sparkles">
    Once your account has been successfully connected, you'll be redirected back to Prizology. From there, you can explore a limited selection of curated prizes. To unlock full access to hundreds of prizes and awards, upgrade to Premium.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email Sign-Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Head to our sign-up page">
    Go to https://prizology.net/login to start the sign-up process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Enter the email you wish to be associated with your account and press "One-Click Access".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    If this is your first time using that email, you'll receive an invitation to activate your account—just click the activation button in the email.

    <Warning>
      The activation email may take up to 5 minutes to arrive. Please check your inbox and be patient while it comes through.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Prizology" icon="sparkles">
    Once activated you'll be redirected back to Prizology. From there, you can explore a limited selection of curated prizes. To unlock full access to hundreds of prizes and awards, upgrade to Premium.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Login

To log in to Prizology, just follow the same steps as signing up.

- **Email:** you’ll receive a secure login link in your inbox.
- **Google:** you’ll be redirected automatically once you select your account.
