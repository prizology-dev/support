## How to manage your premium subscription

For any inquiries regarding your premium subscription, please contact our support team directly.

Send an email to [support@prizology.net](mailto:support@prizology.net) with your inquiry, and our team will assist you with:

- Subscription changes or cancellations
- Billing questions
- Feature access issues
- Account upgrades or downgrades
- Any other premium-related concerns

We will respond as soon as possible.
