## How to purchase premium

Purchase Premium by logging in, visiting the [Pricing page](https://www.prizology.net/pricing), and selecting your plan. You'll be taken to Stripe to complete a secure checkout.

<Card title="Pricing" icon="sparkles" iconType="duotone" href="https://www.prizology.net/pricing" cta="Explore Plans">
  Supercharge your portfolio and help yourself stand out
</Card>

### How to apply a discount or promotion code

At checkout, click "Add promotion code", enter your code, and press "Apply". The discount will be reflected in your total before payment.

![61_1x_shots_so.png](/images/61_1x_shots_so.png)

### Which payment methods are accepted?

Stripe accepts most major debit and credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. See a [full list](https://docs.stripe.com/payments/payment-methods/overview) of Stripe's supported payment methods.
